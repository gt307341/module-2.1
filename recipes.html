Peanut Butter Cups

Ingredients Directions
Peanut Butter Put equal parts of peanut butter and powdered sugar into a bowl. Mix until combined.
Powdered Sugar Heat chocolate in microwave for 2 30-second intervals. Mix until it has become a smooth liquid.
Chocolate Place cupcake liners in a muffin tin and layer chocolate on the bottom, the peanut butter mix in the center, and chocolate again on top.
Refrigerate for 4 hours or until firm.
